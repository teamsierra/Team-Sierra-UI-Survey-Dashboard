Key Design Takeaways
What to prioritise
Readability above all — scored 4.5/5, the highest of any metric. Font legibility is a hard constraint, not a style choice.

Make the certificate moment special — badges & achievements also scored 4.5/5. The completion screen should feel like the peak of the experience.

Left sidebar + bottom tab bar — clear winners for desktop and mobile nav respectively.
What users said
"Make the certificate screen feel like the most special moment in the entire platform."
"Simple, minimalist and easy to navigate."
"It feels like someone actually thought about me when they designed it."
